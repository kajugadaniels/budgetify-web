## Design Context

### Users
Budgetify is for people managing personal cash flow across salary, side income,
daily spending, and planned purchases. They use it repeatedly throughout the
week on mobile and desktop to log money quickly, understand what changed, and
stay in control of the month.

### Brand Personality
Calm, premium, focused. The product should feel trustworthy and composed rather
than loud, playful, or aggressively fintech.

### Aesthetic Direction
Dark-first interface with warm sand-gold accents, tinted neutrals, rounded
geometry, and restrained glass surfaces. The web experience should mirror the
existing Flutter app rather than introducing a separate visual language. Avoid
neon palettes, glossy gradients, noisy charts, and generic SaaS dashboards.

### Design Principles
- Put the next financial decision in front of the user quickly.
- Keep forms lightweight and obvious so logging data feels fast.
- Use glass, blur, and accent color as emphasis, not decoration.
- Favor confident summaries and clear labels over dense analytics.
- Make mobile and desktop feel equivalent, not like one is a cut-down version
  of the other.
